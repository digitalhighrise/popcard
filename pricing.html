Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your account settings in one click. You'll keep Pro features until the end of the current billing period.

What counts as a popcard?

One popcard is one source you "pop" — a YouTube video, an article, a block of notes. Each pop generates a deck of multiple cards. Free includes 10 pops per month; Pro is unlimited.

Do you offer student discounts?

Verified students get 50% off Pro. Reach out from your school email after signing up and we'll apply the discount.

How is my data used?

Your content is yours. We don't sell data, don't train models on your popcards, and use privacy-friendly analytics (Vercel Web Analytics) with no cookies.
